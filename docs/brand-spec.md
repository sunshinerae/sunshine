# The Sunshine Effect — Brand Spec

## Brand Name
**The Sunshine Effect**

## Core Tagline
**Glow from the heart.**

## Brand Essence
A warm, grounded space that helps women move from burnout to alignment — with simple rituals, clarity, and connection.

---

## The Vibe

- Radiant, feminine, and calming
- Warm like golden hour
- Soft but powerful
- Nurturing, not pushy
- Elevated, aesthetic, and intentional
- "Come home to yourself" energy
- Community-led, not performance-led

---

## What Sunshine Stands For

- Self-trust over self-doubt
- Peace as the foundation for growth
- Purpose over proving
- Caring for yourself as a form of service
- Confidence built through imperfect action
- Building a life (and business) that feels aligned, embodied, and alive

---

## Audience

Primarily women in their **20s–30s** (LA/Austin vibe), who:
- Feel meant for more but are unclear where to start
- Are tired of burnout, comparison, and "always on"
- Want aligned friendships and community
- Are drawn to wellness + spirituality + creativity
- Crave structure and momentum without harsh hustle culture

---

## The Transformation

They come in feeling:
- Scattered, burned out, unsure, disconnected

They leave feeling:
- Lighter
- Clearer
- More confident
- More connected
- Proud of who they're becoming

---

## Tone of Voice

- Kind, intimate, and encouraging
- A loving friend who tells the truth gently
- Clear and grounded (not vague)
- Spiritual, but not overly "woo"
- Confident without being salesy
- Simple language, emotional warmth

---

## Key Words & Phrases

- radiance

- pockets of peace
- come home to yourself
- nervous system reset
- self-trust
- embodied

- connected
- gentle momentum
- inner fire
- glow from the heart

## Visual Feel

- **Golden warmth** (sun, honey, amber tones)
- **Soft neutrals + deep grounding tones**
- **Floral overlays** for dreamy feminine texture
- Minimal, clean layouts with romantic details
- Typography mix: elegant display + soft modern support

---

## Color Palette

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Power Purple | `#6E054D` | Primary hero/section backgrounds, CTAs |
| Molten Orange | `#D4510B` | Accent sections, hover states |
| Yellow Sun | `#FFC619` | Secondary backgrounds, highlights |
| Blue Sky | `#95D7E6` | Accent UI elements only |
| White Cloud | `#FCF6F2` | Clean sections, body backgrounds |
| Deep Brown | `#240D01` | Primary text color |

### Contrast Rules

**On Power Purple backgrounds:**
- Text: White Cloud only
- Buttons/accents: Blue Sky or Yellow Sun

**On Yellow Sun backgrounds:**
- Text: Deep Brown or Power Purple
- NEVER white text on yellow

**On White Cloud backgrounds:**
- Text: Deep Brown
- Accents: Power Purple, Molten Orange

---

## Typography

| Role | Font | Style |
|------|------|-------|
| Headlines (H1, H3) | Belvare | Serif, uppercase, bold |
| Subheads (H2, H4) | Laro Soft Bold | Rounded sans, bold |
| Body | Poppins | Sans-serif, regular |

### Typography Rules
- Headlines: uppercase where appropriate
- Body: centered or left-aligned (never fully justified)
- Relaxed line-height throughout
- Generous spacing for "pockets of peace"

---

## Button Style

- Pill-shaped (rounded-full)
- High contrast text
- Common labels: "Glow," "Sign Up," "Join The List," "Work With Sunshine," "I Want In"
- Microcopy near CTAs to reduce decision anxiety

---

## Animation Guidelines

| Content Type | Animation Style |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Body content | Soft fades, slow easing (0.6s+), gentle movement |
| CTAs & cards | Crisp motion, defined timing (0.3s), confident |
| Page sections | Fade/slide in on scroll |
| Card grids | Staggered reveal |

---

## What to Avoid

- Overly corporate language
- Aggressive hustle messaging
- Too much vagueness ("alignment" with no outcome)
- Trauma-heavy positioning (keep it safe + opt-in)
- Anything that feels chaotic or cluttered
- White text on yellow backgrounds
- Long blocks of all-caps body text

---

## Key Catchphrases

Use these throughout the site:

- "Discipline is self-love in motion"
- "Move like it's already yours"
- "You're allowed to want more ease"
- "Radiance is yours"
- "Real power doesn't have to push or prove"

## Event Types

### Golden Hour
- High energy, heart-led networking
- Mindset coaching, storytelling
- Keywords: electric, heart-led, connective, playful
- Visual: warm colors (orange, yellow), sun-drenched imagery

### Lunar Room
- Slow, introspective, restorative
- Yoga, sound, meditation
- Keywords: exhale, grounded, gentle, inward
- Visual: cooler palette (purple, blue), moon/candle imagery
